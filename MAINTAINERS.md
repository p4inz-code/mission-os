## Future Maintainers

As the project grows, additional maintainers may be appointed for specific areas, including:

- Core System
- Desktop Experience
- Security
- Documentation
- Quality Assurance
- Community Management
- Infrastructure

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review pull requests
- Uphold project standards
- Mentor contributors
- Help plan releases
- Maintain a respectful and collaborative community

---

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainers are selected based on:

- Consistent, high-quality contributions
- Technical knowledge
- Collaboration
- Long-term commitment
- Community involvement

There is no fixed application process. Maintainers are invited as the project evolves.
