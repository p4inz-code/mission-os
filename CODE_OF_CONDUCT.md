# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community.

Everyone participating in Mission OS is expected to contribute to a positive environment regardless of experience, background, or identity.

---

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on the project.
- Help others learn.
- Respect differing viewpoints.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Threats
- Trolling
- Deliberate disruption
- Sharing private information without permission

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include warnings, removal of content, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans depending on severity.

---

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainers.

Every report will be reviewed respectfully and confidentially.

---

Thank you for helping build a welcoming open-source community.
